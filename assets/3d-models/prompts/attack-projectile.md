# Attack Projectile Prompt

## Runtime Asset

`attack-projectile.glb`

## Prompt

Create an original stylized 3D attack projectile for a mobile Battleship-style game. The projectile should look like a compact neon torpedo or energy shell with a dark metallic core, cyan-white front glow, magenta-red rear heat accent, and a short trail anchor. The style is adult animated neo-noir graphic 3D with high contrast, painterly roughness, and ink-like edge accents.

The asset should be simple, fast, and readable during a brief attack animation. It should work as a projectile launched from one board side toward a target cell.

## Requirements

- Small centered projectile model.
- Direction clearly readable.
- Optional attachment point for particle trail.
- Cyan-white leading glow.
- Red-magenta rear accent.
- Mobile-friendly geometry.

## Tripo Settings

Use these Tripo generation settings:

- AI Model: `v3.1 - Best Quality`
- Texture: `On`
- Texture Quality: `2K`
- PBR: `Off`
- Topology: `Triangle`
- Polycount: `600`
- Ultra Mesh Quality: `Off`
- Generate in Parts: `Off`
- 8K Texture: `Off`
- Privacy: `Sharing Only`
- Runtime Target: lightweight mobile WebGL/browser asset with fast loading and low GPU cost

## Negative Prompt

No realistic missile branding, no national markings, no copied sci-fi weapon, no logos, no readable text, no gore, no cluttered background, no overly complex mechanical parts.

## Notes

The projectile should support quick turn feedback, not long cinematic interruption.
