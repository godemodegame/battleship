## Runtime Asset

## Prompt

```text
Create one isolated 2D comic onomatopoeia sprite containing exactly the text
"FWOOSH!" for the curved mid-flight arc of a fast projectile in a mobile
Battleship game. Use an original adult animated neo-noir graphic illustration
style: high-contrast painted-comic rendering, ink-like contours, hand-painted
roughness, dry-brush fibers, subtle halftone erosion, and expressive stepped
animation energy.

Curve the complete word along a shallow rising arc from lower left to upper
right. Stretch the first F into a short launch smear, pull the double O into a
compressed speed tunnel, and taper the final SH toward the projectile. Use a
cold-white #E8F7FF main fill with an electric-cyan #21F4FF outer edge,
signal-amber #FFB000 cuts through the center, tiny hot-magenta #FF2EA6
misregistered accents, and a thick near-black #07080D painted contour. Add two
broken crescent brush streaks that follow the same arc and remain attached to
the word silhouette.

The result must feel fast, elegant, tense, and readable at 120 pixels wide over
the angled top-down 3D board. Transparent RGBA background, generous clear
padding, 1536x768 source canvas, exact spelling "FWOOSH!", no other text.
Original design, not based on an existing comic, show, game, font, logo,
artist, or protected visual identity.
```
