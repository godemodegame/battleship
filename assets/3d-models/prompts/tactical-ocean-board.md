# Tactical Ocean Board Prompt

## Runtime Asset

`tactical-ocean-board.glb`

## Prompt

Create an original 3D model of a mobile-first tactical ocean board for a Battleship-style game. The board is a 10 by 10 grid floating over dark reflective water, with thin electric cyan grid lines, subtle dark glass tiles, and a cinematic neo-noir mood. The style is adult animated graphic 3D with ink-like contours, painterly roughness, high contrast shadows, and controlled neon reflections.

The board should feel like a holographic war table suspended above a surreal night harbor. Each cell must be clearly readable from an angled top-down mobile camera. Add subtle bevels, small glowing corner markers, and a compact outer frame with amber status lights. Keep the center playable and uncluttered.

## Requirements

- 10 by 10 playable grid.
- Clear cell boundaries.
- Dark ocean surface under the grid.
- Cyan neutral grid color.
- Amber selection accent areas.
- Red-magenta impact accent areas can be included as optional material slots.
- No text labels baked into the model.
- Optimized for mobile real-time rendering.

## Negative Prompt

Do not copy any existing show, episode, game board, logo, or protected design. No flat spreadsheet look, no cute toy style, no realistic military simulator, no cluttered UI panels, no national symbols, no text, no heavy decorative background, no single-color purple theme.

## Notes

The board is the main gameplay asset. Readability is more important than visual complexity.
