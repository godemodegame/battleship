# BLAM Hit Sprite Prompt

## Runtime Asset

`comic-sfx-blam.png`

## Prompt

```text
Create one isolated 2D comic onomatopoeia result sprite containing exactly the
text "BLAM!" as an alternate successful-hit accent in a mobile Battleship
game. Use an original adult animated neo-noir graphic illustration style with
a high-contrast painted-comic finish, strong ink-like contours, hand-painted
roughness, dry-brush texture, subtle halftone breakup, and punchy stepped
animation energy.

Compose the word on a slight upward diagonal inside a broken angular starburst.
Give the B and L a heavy compressed impact weight, make the A the bright center
of the collision, and throw the M and exclamation mark outward with two sharp
motion cuts. Use hot magenta #FF2EA6 as the dominant color, impact red #FF3B30
on the lower shadow planes, cold white #E8F7FF at the center, a restrained
electric-cyan #21F4FF counter-edge, and a thick near-black #07080D ink outline.
Add sparse triangular spark shards and visible brush drag at the edges.

The sprite must be bold and readable on a portrait phone over the existing hit
flash. Transparent RGBA background, 1024x1024 source canvas, exact spelling
"BLAM!", no other text. Original design, not based on an existing comic, show,
game, font, logo, artist, or protected visual identity.
```
