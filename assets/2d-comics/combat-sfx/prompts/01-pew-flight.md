# PEW Flight Sprite Prompt

## Runtime Asset

`comic-sfx-pew.png`

## Prompt

```text
Create one isolated 2D comic onomatopoeia sprite containing exactly the text
"PEW!" for the launch and early flight of a fast projectile in a mobile
Battleship game. Use an original adult animated neo-noir graphic illustration
style: high-contrast painted-comic finish, thick ink-like contours,
hand-painted dry-brush texture variation, sparse halftone grain, and sharp
stepped-animation energy.

Compose the word as a narrow forward-leaning horizontal shape traveling from
left to right. Stretch the P and E slightly into the launch direction, taper
the W into two sharp speed cuts, and make the exclamation mark a small
projectile-like needle. Use a cold-white #E8F7FF letter core, signal-amber
#FFB000 inner streaks, restrained electric-cyan #21F4FF edge separation, and
a thick near-black #07080D painted outline. Add three short broken speed lines
behind the word, attached to the silhouette rather than floating far away.

The sprite must read instantly at 96 pixels wide over dark glass water and
bright 3D effects. Transparent RGBA background, generous clear padding,
1536x768 source canvas, one centered isolated asset, exact spelling "PEW!",
no other text. Original design, not based on an existing comic, show, game,
font, logo, artist, or protected visual identity.
```
