# SPLASH Miss Sprite Prompt

## Runtime Asset

`comic-sfx-splash.png`

## Prompt

```text
Create one isolated 2D comic onomatopoeia result sprite containing exactly the
text "SPLASH!" as the clean missed-shot result in a mobile Battleship game.
Use an original adult animated neo-noir graphic illustration
style with a high-contrast painted-comic finish, ink-like contours,
hand-painted roughness, dry-brush water shapes, subtle halftone erosion, and
fast stepped-animation energy.

Set the word on a shallow upward arc above a compact cold-water crown. Stretch
the S into a curved ripple, keep every letter highly readable, and let the H
and exclamation mark end in two narrow droplets. Use electric cyan #21F4FF and
cold white #E8F7FF as the dominant colors, deep petrol #16242A in the lower
shadow shapes, tiny restrained signal-amber #FFB000 fragments from the spent
projectile, and a thick near-black #07080D ink contour. Add three angular
splash blades, a broken elliptical ripple, and sparse mist dots close to the
word. It must read as water, never as an explosion.

The sprite should feel cooler and calmer than hit effects while remaining
instantly legible on a phone. Transparent RGBA background, 1024x1024 source
canvas, exact spelling "SPLASH!", no other text. Original design, not based on
an existing comic, show, game, font, logo, artist, or protected visual
identity.
```
