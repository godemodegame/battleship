# KABOOM Sunk Sprite Prompt

## Runtime Asset

## Prompt

```text
Create one isolated 2D comic onomatopoeia result sprite containing exactly the
text "KABOOM!" as the final ship-destruction result in a mobile Battleship
game. Use an original adult animated neo-noir graphic illustration
style: high-contrast painted-comic finish, massive ink-like contours,
hand-painted material breakup, cracked dry-brush texture, selective halftone
and hatch details, and decisive stepped-animation energy.

Build the heaviest silhouette in the combat SFX family. Arrange the word on a
slight downward diagonal as if a structure is breaking and submerging. Fracture
the K and A with sharp red-white fissures, keep both O letters fully readable,
and pull the final M and exclamation mark downward into broken debris cuts. Use
impact red #FF3B30 and hot magenta #FF2EA6 as dominant colors, cold white
#E8F7FF in the central break, electric cyan #21F4FF on two receding grid-like
edge fragments, deep petrol #16242A in the lower wreck shadows, and a very
thick near-black #07080D painted contour. Add a compact crown of shard debris,
two black smoke wedges, red-cyan crack lines, and one broken cyan ripple below
the word. Keep all accents attached close to the lettering.

The sprite must communicate finality and a destroyed ship without showing a
literal ship or revealing hidden cells. Transparent RGBA background,
1024x1024 source canvas, exact spelling "KABOOM!", no other text. Original
design, not based on an existing comic, show, game, font, logo, artist, or
protected visual identity.
```
