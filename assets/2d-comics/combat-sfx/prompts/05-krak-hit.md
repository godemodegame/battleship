## Prompt

```text
Create one isolated 2D comic onomatopoeia result sprite containing exactly the
text "KRAK!" as the primary successful-impact result in a mobile Battleship
game. Use an original adult animated neo-noir graphic
illustration style: high-contrast painted-comic finish, bold ink-like
contours, hand-painted material breakup, dry-brush roughness, selective
halftone and hatch texture, and sharp stepped-animation timing.

Build a compact asymmetric impact burst around the lettering. Make the K
strike like two crossing metal shards, fracture the R and A with controlled
crack cuts, and kick the final K outward into the exclamation mark. Use impact
red #FF3B30 and hot magenta #FF2EA6 as the main fill, cold-white #E8F7FF on
the hardest center cuts, a thin electric-cyan #21F4FF shock edge, and a thick
near-black #07080D ink contour. Add five short shard-like sparks and two
painted black smoke tears, all attached close to the word.

The silhouette must read immediately as a successful direct hit without
covering more than roughly a 3x3 board-cell area. Transparent RGBA background,
1024x1024 source canvas, exact spelling "KRAK!", no other text. Original
design, not based on an existing comic, show, game, font, logo, artist, or
protected visual identity.
```
